---
title: Dropdown
description: A toggleable menu of actions or links, anchored to a trigger and positioned by Radix UI.
---

### Default

Compose a `DropdownMenu` from a `DropdownMenuTrigger` (rendered as a `Button`
via `asChild`) and a `DropdownMenuContent` holding a label, items, and a
separator. The menu opens on click and handles keyboard navigation for you.

<DropdownMenu>
  <DropdownMenuTrigger asChild>
    <Button>Open menu</Button>
  </DropdownMenuTrigger>
  <DropdownMenuContent className="min-w-48">
    <DropdownMenuLabel>My account</DropdownMenuLabel>
    <DropdownMenuItem>Profile</DropdownMenuItem>
    <DropdownMenuItem>Settings</DropdownMenuItem>
    <DropdownMenuItem>Billing</DropdownMenuItem>
    <DropdownMenuSeparator />
    <DropdownMenuItem>Sign out</DropdownMenuItem>
  </DropdownMenuContent>
</DropdownMenu>

### Placement

Position is controlled by Radix UI through the `side` (`"top" | "right" |
"bottom" | "left"`) and `align` (`"start" | "center" | "end"`) props on
`DropdownMenuContent` — not the Bootstrap direction classes. Here the menu is
end-aligned and opens above the trigger.

<DropdownMenu>
  <DropdownMenuTrigger asChild>
    <Button color="secondary">Aligned menu</Button>
  </DropdownMenuTrigger>
  <DropdownMenuContent side="top" align="end" className="min-w-48">
    <DropdownMenuItem>Move up</DropdownMenuItem>
    <DropdownMenuItem>Move down</DropdownMenuItem>
    <DropdownMenuSeparator />
    <DropdownMenuItem disabled>Archive (disabled)</DropdownMenuItem>
  </DropdownMenuContent>
</DropdownMenu>

### Non-interactive text

Use `DropdownMenuItemText` for plain, non-actionable copy inside the menu, and
`DropdownMenuLabel` for a section header.

<DropdownMenu>
  <DropdownMenuTrigger asChild>
    <Button variant="outline">Details</Button>
  </DropdownMenuTrigger>
  <DropdownMenuContent className="min-w-56">
    <DropdownMenuLabel>Signed in as</DropdownMenuLabel>
    <DropdownMenuItemText>sohel@onesuite.io</DropdownMenuItemText>
    <DropdownMenuSeparator />
    <DropdownMenuItem>Account settings</DropdownMenuItem>
  </DropdownMenuContent>
</DropdownMenu>
