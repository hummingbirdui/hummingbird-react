---
title: Drawer
description: A draggable, dismissible panel that slides in from any edge — built on Vaul with Bootstrap's offcanvas styling.
---

### Default

Compose a `Drawer` from a `DrawerTrigger` (rendered as a `Button` via `asChild`)
and a `DrawerContent`. The content holds a `DrawerHeader` (with `DrawerTitle`
and a `DrawerClose`) and a `DrawerBody`. The panel slides in from the bottom by
default, the backdrop fades, and a drag handle lets you flick it closed. Focus
trapping, scroll locking, drag-to-dismiss, and `Esc`/outside-click dismissal are
handled by [Vaul](https://vaul.emilkowal.ski/).

<Drawer>
  <DrawerTrigger asChild>
    <Button>Open drawer</Button>
  </DrawerTrigger>
  <DrawerContent>
    <DrawerHeader>
      <DrawerTitle>Drawer title</DrawerTitle>
      <DrawerClose asChild>
        <CloseButton />
      </DrawerClose>
    </DrawerHeader>
    <DrawerBody>
      <DrawerDescription>
        This is a drawer. Drag it down, click the backdrop, press Escape, or use
        a close button to dismiss it.
      </DrawerDescription>
    </DrawerBody>
  </DrawerContent>
</Drawer>

### Direction

Pass `direction` (`"top" | "bottom" | "left" | "right"`) to `Drawer` to choose
the edge the panel slides from. Each direction maps to the matching Bootstrap
offcanvas placement (`.offcanvas-start`, `.offcanvas-end`, `.offcanvas-top`,
`.offcanvas-bottom`); left/right drawers span the full height, top/bottom take
the configured offcanvas height.

### Scrollable body

`DrawerBody` (`.offcanvas-body`) grows to fill the panel and scrolls its own
overflow, so the header stays pinned while a long body scrolls.

<Drawer direction="right">
  <DrawerTrigger asChild>
    <Button color="secondary">Open long drawer</Button>
  </DrawerTrigger>
  <DrawerContent>
    <DrawerHeader>
      <DrawerTitle>Terms of service</DrawerTitle>
      <DrawerClose asChild>
        <CloseButton />
      </DrawerClose>
    </DrawerHeader>
