---
title: Drawer
description: A draggable, dismissible panel that slides in from any edge of the screen.
---

## Default

The drawer slides in from the bottom edge and dismisses on drag, backdrop click, or Escape.

<ComponentPreview name="drawer-default" />

## Direction

Passing `direction` (`"top" | "right" | "bottom" | "left"`) to `Drawer` sets the edge the panel slides from.

<ComponentPreview name="drawer-direction" />

## Without overlay

Setting `overlay={false}` on `DrawerContent` renders the panel without the backdrop.

<ComponentPreview name="drawer-without-overlay" />

## Scrollable body

`DrawerBody` grows to fill the panel and scrolls its own overflow while the header stays pinned.

<ComponentPreview name="drawer-scrollable" />
