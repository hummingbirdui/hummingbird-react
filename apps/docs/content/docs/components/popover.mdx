---
title: Popover
description: A floating panel anchored to a trigger — built on Radix Popover with Bootstrap's popover styling.
---

### Default

Compose a `Popover` from a `PopoverTrigger` (rendered as a `Button` via
`asChild`) and a `PopoverContent`. The content holds a `PopoverHeader` and a
`PopoverBody`. Radix handles focus management, positioning, and
`Esc`/outside-click dismissal; the panel fades and scales from the trigger.

<Popover>
  <PopoverTrigger asChild>
    <Button>Open popover</Button>
  </PopoverTrigger>
  <PopoverContent>
    <PopoverHeader>Popover title</PopoverHeader>
    <PopoverBody>
      And here's some amazing content. It's very engaging. Right?
    </PopoverBody>
  </PopoverContent>
</Popover>

### Placement

Pass `side` (`"top" | "right" | "bottom" | "left"`) and `align`
(`"start" | "center" | "end"`) to `PopoverContent` to choose where the panel
opens relative to the trigger.

<div className="flex flex-wrap gap-2">
  <Popover>
    <PopoverTrigger asChild>
      <Button variant="outline">Top</Button>
    </PopoverTrigger>
    <PopoverContent side="top">
      <PopoverBody>Opens above the trigger.</PopoverBody>
    </PopoverContent>
  </Popover>

  <Popover>
    <PopoverTrigger asChild>
      <Button variant="outline">Right</Button>
    </PopoverTrigger>
    <PopoverContent side="right">
      <PopoverBody>Opens to the right.</PopoverBody>
    </PopoverContent>
  </Popover>

  <Popover>
    <PopoverTrigger asChild>
      <Button variant="outline">Bottom</Button>
    </PopoverTrigger>
    <PopoverContent side="bottom">
      <PopoverBody>Opens below the trigger.</PopoverBody>
    </PopoverContent>
  </Popover>

  <Popover>
    <PopoverTrigger asChild>
      <Button variant="outline">Left</Button>
    </PopoverTrigger>
    <PopoverContent side="left">
      <PopoverBody>Opens to the left.</PopoverBody>
    </PopoverContent>
  </Popover>
</div>

### With a close button

Use `PopoverClose` (rendered as a `CloseButton` via `asChild`) inside the
content to dismiss the popover from within.

<Popover>
  <PopoverTrigger asChild>
    <Button color="secondary">Dismissable</Button>
  </PopoverTrigger>
  <PopoverContent>
    <PopoverHeader className="flex items-center justify-between">
      Heads up
      <PopoverClose asChild>
        <CloseButton />
      </PopoverClose>
    </PopoverHeader>
    <PopoverBody>Click the close button to dismiss this popover.</PopoverBody>
  </PopoverContent>
</Popover>
