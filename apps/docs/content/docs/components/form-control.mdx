---
title: Form Control
description: Text inputs, textareas, labels, and helper text for building forms.
---

### Default

`FormControl` renders a styled `<input>`.

<div className="flex flex-col gap-2 max-w-sm my-4">
  <FormControl placeholder="Enter your name" />
</div>

### Variants

`outline` (default) is bordered; `fill` uses a filled, muted surface.

<div className="flex flex-col gap-2 max-w-sm my-4">
  <FormControl variant="outline" placeholder="Outline" />
  <FormControl variant="fill" placeholder="Fill" />
</div>

### Sizes

Use `size` to render `sm`, `md` (default), or `lg`.

<div className="flex flex-col gap-2 max-w-sm my-4">
  <FormControl size="sm" placeholder="Small" />
  <FormControl size="md" placeholder="Medium" />
  <FormControl size="lg" placeholder="Large" />
</div>

### Colors

`color` sets the focus accent (and file-button color).

<div className="flex flex-col gap-2 max-w-sm my-4">
  <FormControl color="primary" placeholder="Primary" />
  <FormControl color="success" placeholder="Success" />
  <FormControl color="warning" placeholder="Warning" />
</div>

### Field with label and helper text

Compose `FormField`, `FormLabel`, `FormControl`, and `FormText`. The label
recolors to match the control on focus.

<div className="max-w-sm my-4">
  <FormField>
    <FormLabel htmlFor="email">Email</FormLabel>
    <FormControl id="email" type="email" placeholder="you@example.com" />
    <FormText>We'll never share your email.</FormText>
  </FormField>
</div>

### Validation state

Set `state` to `valid` or `invalid`; `invalid` also sets `aria-invalid`. Pair
with a `FormText` of the matching `variant`.

<div className="max-w-sm my-4">
  <FormField>
    <FormLabel htmlFor="username">Username</FormLabel>
    <FormControl id="username" state="invalid" defaultValue="bad name" />
    <FormText variant="invalid">Usernames cannot contain spaces.</FormText>
  </FormField>
</div>

### With icons

Wrap the control in `InputIcon` and add an `InputIconStart` and/or
`InputIconEnd`. The wrapper pads the control to make room.

<div className="flex flex-col gap-3 max-w-sm my-4">
  <InputIcon>
    <InputIconStart>@</InputIconStart>
    <FormControl placeholder="Username" />
  </InputIcon>
  <InputIcon>
    <FormControl type="search" placeholder="Search…" />
    <InputIconEnd>⌕</InputIconEnd>
  </InputIcon>
  <InputIcon>
    <InputIconStart>$</InputIconStart>
    <FormControl placeholder="0.00" />
    <InputIconEnd>USD</InputIconEnd>
  </InputIcon>
</div>

### Textarea

`Textarea` shares the same styling for multi-line input.

<div className="max-w-sm my-4">
  <Textarea rows={4} placeholder="Write a short bio…" />
</div>

### Disabled

<div className="flex flex-col gap-2 max-w-sm my-4">
  <FormControl placeholder="Disabled" disabled />
</div>
